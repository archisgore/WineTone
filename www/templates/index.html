How it works

  1. Sign in. Email, Google, or GitHub. Your labels live under your real (Clerk-managed) account, so usernames are unique and only you can edit your own profile.

  3. We fit your projection. ~milliseconds on {{ backend_label }}. Your vocabulary becomes a linear map into the catalog's 384-dim embedding space.
