How it works

  1. Sign in. You can use email, Google, or GitHub. Your labels live under your account, so usernames remain unique and only you can edit your own profile.
  2. Label five or more wines you know. Search the catalog by producer, region, or grape, and describe each one in whatever words make sense to you. Both positive and negative descriptions are useful signal.
  3. We fit your projection. The system computes the fit in a few milliseconds on {{ backend_label }}. Your vocabulary becomes a linear map into the catalog's 384-dimensional embedding space.
  4. Ask in plain English. The same query that returned generic results before calibration begins to return wines that match your meaning of the words.
