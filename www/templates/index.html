How it works

  1. Scan or search. Point your camera at a label, or search by producer, region, or grape.
  2. Describe what you know. Label five or more wines in whatever words make sense to you. Positive and negative both help.
  3. WineTone learns your vocabulary. The system fits a personal map across the catalog's embedding space in milliseconds.
  4. Ask naturally. “Something like Barolo but less tannic.” “Funky and orange but not too weird.” It searches for your meaning, not the literal words.
