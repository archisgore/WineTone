How it works

  1. Sign in with email, Google, or GitHub. Your labels live under your real (Clerk-managed) account, so usernames are unique and only you can edit your own profile.
  2. Label 5+ wines you've tried — search the catalog by producer, region, or grape, then describe each in whatever words make sense to you.
  3. WineTone fits a personal projection from your vocabulary into the catalog's embedding space. Running on {{ backend_label }}. ~milliseconds to fit.
  4. Ask for a wine in your own words. The same query that returns generic results before calibration shifts to wines that match your meaning of the words after.
