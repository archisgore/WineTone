You'll calibrate the model with five-ish wines you've tried, in your own words. Once you've trained your palate, ask for recommendations in plain English and see what shifts.

How it works

  1. You label 5+ wines you've tried — search the catalog by producer, region, or grape, then describe each in whatever words make sense to you.
  2. WineTone fits a personal projection from your vocabulary into the catalog's embedding space. Running on {{ backend_label }}. ~milliseconds to fit.
  3. Ask for a wine in your own words. The same query that returns generic results before calibration shifts to wines that match your meaning of the words after.
